Our Story & Storytellers

P&H Consult was born out of a mission to ensure that development works for the people. We believe that true impact is created through justice-centered consultancy and inclusive growth.

Our journey began with a focus on bridging the gap in public health and governance, and has since expanded into a multi-sector force across Sierra Leone, Kenya, and beyond.
